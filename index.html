Our Story

Hardware House was founded in 1973 by our family on the principles of trust, quality, and fair pricing. Starting as a small hardware shop on Jawahar Marg, we have grown to become one of Indore's most respected aluminium product suppliers.

Today we serve thousands of customers across Madhya Pradesh, offering the widest range of aluminium sections, sheets, and hardware accessories at competitive market prices.
